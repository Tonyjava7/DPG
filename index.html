DPG

Daniel Prayer Garden was formed with the belief that all Christians need a place to gather, prayer, worship, and to retreat in order to connect more deeply and richly with Jesus and each other.

The busy world can choke the life out of any person. DPG is the perfect place for church groups to meet and de-stress with God.

Our greatest joy is derived from moments of silence with God.
